Student

My name is Helena Spencer and I am currently a Junior majoring in Information Systems and double minoring in Human Computer Interactions (HCI) and Societal and Human Impacts on Future Technologies (SHIFT) at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU). I not only play for the varsity women's soccer team, but also represent the entire CMU student-athlete population through my position as President of the Student-Athlete Advisory Council (SAAC) and University Leadership Student Advisory Council (ULSAC).

As for my academic skill set, over the past couple of years I have been heavily involved with the human centered software development process and gained numerous tools and techniques to grasp a better understanding of clientele. These tools include creating mockups, story boards, style guides, wireframes, affinity diagrams, journey maps, and various fidelity prototypes.
